# Near Future Tech

Near Future Tech is a content mod for Catacylsm Bright Nights. 

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What does the mod add?
A bunch of items, as well as some recipes. The main goal is to increase variety of available equipment, while bridging the gap between time-stopping bionics and modern assault rifles. 
It's mostly hard sci-fi stuff, and some minor obscure guns from irl. 
Overall I try to keep the tone and balance of vanilla unchanged. 

### I want to suggest something, will you add it?
Suggestions are always very welcome! Let me know your idea and we can talk about it. No promises though.

### I want to write a commit, what content can I add?
Anything that would not feel out of place in any of the following: 
- Menace 
- The Expanse 
- Ghost in the Shell 
- Blindsight 
- Brigador

To be more descriptive, items and techs that feel plausible, and either already exist, are in the experimental phase, or could feasibly exist in a few decades / centuries. 
I will not add items that: 
- Violate conservation of matter or energy (the blob does that enough)
- Conflict heavily with the lore (eg. alien-made guns)
- Are really bad for game balance 

### Why didn't you simply add this content to vanilla?
The community's consensus is that sci-fi and modern content should not mix within the game. So while Rivtech guns and modern assault rifle can coexist, fictional ammo for real guns is not allowed. 
If you want to port any content, or try to get the mod in the official default repo, you are welcome!

## Requirements
Near Future Tech requires the latest experimental of Cataclysm Bright Nights. You will also need to enable the mod "Exotic Ammo Types", which comes by default with BN.
In the future, Otopack 2 will also be a requirement for sound effects. Right now gun's reloads, for example, are silent. 

## Road Map and content

- [x] Bolter

A ficitional 40mm bolter inspired by warhammer. Diegetically it's a cosplay item that got marketed as a nerf gun, but it's an open secret it can fit 40mm grenades. 
A  militarised version exists, it shoots 40x53mm grenades and is issued to power armor soldiers. 

- [x] PKM

Real, Soviet 7.62x39mm machine gun. Heavy, but shoots a very big round in full auto. 

- [X] 762x39mm air burst round

A fictional Korean round, high flesh damage but useless against armor. Inspired by the movie Elysium.
